The survey ship found the silent moon orbiting a star that should have died centuries ago. Beneath its glassy plains, Mara detected a pulse repeating her own childhood nickname.

She followed the signal into a buried chamber where a machine dreamed possible futures. It had kept the star alive while waiting for the one person who could choose which future became real.

Mara refused every shining prophecy and switched the machine off. As darkness crossed the moon, the ancient star finally bloomed into a nebula, scattering new worlds into the waiting sky.
