AT MIDNIGHT, THE OBSERVATORY RECEIVED A REPLY FROM A STAR THAT HAD VANISHED CENTURIES AGO. THE MESSAGE CONTAINED ONLY MARA'S NAME AND TOMORROW'S DATE.

SHE AIMED THE QUANTUM TELESCOPE AT THE EMPTY COORDINATES AND SAW HERSELF STANDING ON A SILVER SHORE BENEATH TWO MOONS. HER FUTURE REFLECTION RAISED A HAND AND WARNED, "DO NOT ANSWER TWICE."

BEHIND MARA, THE TRANSMITTER CHIMED AGAIN. SHE ERASED THE PREPARED RESPONSE, BUT ACROSS THE DEAD STAR'S DISTANCE, THE WOMAN ON THE SILVER SHORE SMILED AS IF THE SECOND ANSWER HAD ALREADY ARRIVED.
