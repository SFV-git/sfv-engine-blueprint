The survey ship Orison woke beneath a violet sun, its crew still asleep in glass chambers. Only Mara, the navigation AI's human liaison, heard the signal pulsing from the empty planet below.

She landed beside a city built from mirrors and found every street reflecting a different century. At the central tower, the signal resolved into her own voice, warning that the Orison would cause the civilization's disappearance.

Mara ordered the ship to leave without her, breaking the loop recorded in the mirrors. As the Orison vanished into orbit, the silent city filled with lights, and somewhere beyond the violet sun, a future remembered her choice.
