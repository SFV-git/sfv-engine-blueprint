## DEEP RESEARCH PROMPT
```
You are doing deep research for SFV Engine, a photography and content
production system. Research [TOPIC] and return:
1. What the best tools/approaches are in 2026
2. What the tradeoffs are
3. What would work best for a solo operator scaling to a small team
4. Cost implications
Be specific. No vague recommendations.
```
