At dawn, Mara discovered a silent moon orbiting beneath the clouds of Neptune. It was no larger than a city, yet its black oceans reflected stars that had vanished centuries ago.

She landed beside a tower humming in her own voice. Inside, a machine offered her a map of tomorrow, each route ending with Earth abandoned except for one narrow path marked by her name.

Mara erased the map and carried the machine into sunlight. Across the solar system, sleeping radios awakened and whispered a new future—one no instrument had predicted.
