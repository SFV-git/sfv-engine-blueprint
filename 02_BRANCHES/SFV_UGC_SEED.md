# SFV UGC — WILL'S ACTUAL BUSINESS (SEED DOC)

## Client Acquisition Channels (Will-confirmed)
1. Direct outreach to known contacts who do UGC for brands — first channel, already warm
2. Scraper bot to find all local UGC creators offering this as a service → get in same rooms,
   pitch in person
3. Sales team doing outreach to US clients and other Canadian provinces
4. Website: companies can book a digital rebrand (uses the full AIO UGC pipeline) + partnership
   with a web developer friend for bundled deal (shoot fees + website build)

## What SFV Actually Does as a UGC Provider
- Will is the creator/shooter — not aggregating UGC from consumers, not running campaigns
- Clients are brands/companies/businesses
- Product = short-form video content (reels) on retainer
- Pipeline: outreach → pitch → brief → shoot → edit → deliver → invoice → repeat

## Website Product Tiers (Will-confirmed)
Four distinct user-facing sides:
1. Client side (brand books and manages their content)
2. True admin side (Will's full control layer)
3. SFV subcontractor side (field operators using the SFV system)
4. SaaS client + admin side (if/when productized for other shooters)
