AT MIDNIGHT, THE OBSERVATORY RECEIVED A MESSAGE FROM A STAR THAT HAD VANISHED A CENTURY AGO. IT CONTAINED ONLY A MAP OF EARTH AND A BLINKING MARK BENEATH THE PACIFIC.

DR. IMANI DESCENDED IN A PRESSURE SPHERE AND FOUND A SILVER DOOR STANDING UPRIGHT ON THE OCEAN FLOOR. WHEN SHE TOUCHED IT, THE DOOR OPENED ONTO A SUNLIT CITY WHERE MACHINES REMEMBERED EVERY HUMAN DREAM.

THE MACHINES OFFERED TO RETURN ONE DREAM TO THE WAKING WORLD. IMANI CHOSE THE OLDEST: HUMANITY LOOKING UP AT THE SKY WITHOUT FEAR, AND WHEN SHE SURFACED, THE VANISHED STAR WAS SHINING AGAIN.
