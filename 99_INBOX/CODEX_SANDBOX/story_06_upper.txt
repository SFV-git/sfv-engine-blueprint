AT DAWN, THE SURVEY SHIP MERIDIAN FOUND A MOON THAT CAST NO SHADOW. ITS SILVER PLAINS REFLECTED STARS THAT HAD VANISHED CENTURIES AGO, ARRANGING THEIR FINAL LIGHT INTO A MAP.

DR. IMANI VALE FOLLOWED THE MAP TO A CRATER WHERE A BLACK SEED HOVERED ABOVE THE DUST. WHEN SHE TOUCHED IT, THE SEED UNFOLDED INTO A TINY DOORWAY, AND THROUGH IT SHE SAW EARTH BENEATH UNFAMILIAR CONSTELLATIONS.

THE DOORWAY OFFERED NO MESSAGE, ONLY A CHOICE. IMANI PLANTED THE SEED BESIDE THE SHIP AND WATCHED A FOREST OF DOORS RISE TOWARD THE SKY, EACH OPENING ONTO A DIFFERENT FUTURE WAITING TO BE EXPLORED.
