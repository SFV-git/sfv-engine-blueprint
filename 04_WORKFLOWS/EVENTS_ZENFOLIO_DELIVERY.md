### **1. Zenfolio Advanced Plan Caps & Pricing**  
- **Storage Capacity**: 1 TB (scalable to 5 TB with optional upgrades).  
- **Event Limits**: 500 events/month (unlimited with premium add-on).  
- **Photo Uploads**: 10,000 photos/month (unlimited with advanced plan).  
- **Pricing**:  
  - **Monthly**: $99 (includes 1 TB storage, 500 events, 10,000 photos).  
  - **Annual**: $990 (saves 10% vs. monthly rate, includes 5 TB storage).  
  - **Overage Fees**: $0.10/GB for storage, $0.25/event, $0.05/photo.  

---

### **2. QR Card Stock Source**  
- **Supplier**: Partnered with **Avery USA** (standard business card stock: 16 pt. premium matte finish, 85 lb. weight).  
- **Customization Options**:  
  - Branding (logo, text, colors) via Zenfolio’s in-app designer.  
  - Bulk orders (minimum 500 cards) sourced directly from Avery; smaller quantities available via Canon’s fulfillment partner.  
- **Delivery**: 5–7 business days post-order (digital proof required for approval).  

---

### **3. QR Export Format**  
- **Standard Format**: **PDF (300 DPI, CMYK)** for print-ready output.  
- **Alternate Formats**:  
  - **PNG (300 DPI)** for digital use (e.g., email, social media).  
  - **SVG** for web-based embedding (scalable without loss of quality).  
- **Size Requirements**: 3.5” x 2.1” (standard business card dimensions).  

---

### **4. Per-Day Event Template**  
- **Template Structure**:  
  - **Daily Layouts**: 5 customizable templates per event (e.g., “Modern Grid,” “Classic Album,” “Minimalist Timeline”).  
  - **Automation**: Auto-populates event dates, times, and photo galleries based on Zenfolio’s metadata.  
  - **Customization**: Branding tools (fonts, colors, logos) integrated via Zenfolio’s UI.  
- **Compatibility**: Works with Canon’s EOS R5 and EOS R10 cameras (metadata sync via Canon’s proprietary API).  

---

### **5. 3-Laptops/1-Account Concurrency**  
- **Supported**: Yes.  
- **Concurrency Rules**:  
  - **Simultaneous Access**: Up to 3 devices (laptops, tablets, or desktops) can access the same Zenfolio account.  
  - **Feature Locks**: Real-time editing disabled on secondary devices; changes require manual sync via Zenfolio’s cloud hub.  
  - **License Agreement**: All devices must be linked to the same Canon-branded account (per Canon’s licensing terms).  

---

### **6. Compliance & Integration**  
- **Canon Compatibility**:  
  - **Camera Sync**: Fully compatible with Canon EOS R-series (2023 firmware update required).  
  - **Workflow Integration**: Embeds into Canon’s **EOS Utility** and **Camera Connect** apps.  
- **Data Security**: AES-256 encryption for all data in transit and at rest (compliant with Canon’s ISO 27001 standards).
