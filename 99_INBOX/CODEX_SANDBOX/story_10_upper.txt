THE SURVEY SHIP FOUND THE SILENT MOON CIRCLING A STAR THAT SHOULD NOT EXIST. BENEATH ITS GLASSY SURFACE, MIRA DETECTED A PULSE REPEATING EVERY TEN SECONDS, STEADY AS A SLEEPING HEART.

SHE LOWERED A PROBE INTO A FISSURE, AND THE MOON ANSWERED BY PROJECTING A MAP ACROSS THE COCKPIT. IT SHOWED HUMANITY'S OLDEST RADIO SIGNALS SPREADING THROUGH SPACE, FOLLOWED BY A SINGLE MESSAGE: WE HEARD YOU.

BEFORE MIRA COULD CALL EARTH, THE IMPOSSIBLE STAR OPENED LIKE AN EYE. THE MOON'S PULSE QUICKENED, HER ENGINES CAME ALIVE WITHOUT COMMAND, AND THE SHIP TURNED TOWARD A DOORWAY MADE OF LIGHT.
